Spaghetti Bolognese

Cook ground beef with diced onions and garlic in a pan until browned. Add canned crushed tomatoes and Italian seasoning. Simmer for 20 minutes and serve over cooked spaghetti.

Chicken Stir-Fry

Saut√© sliced chicken in a pan until cooked. Add your favorite stir-fry vegetables (like bell peppers and broccoli). Pour stir-fry sauce over the mixture and cook until veggies are tender. Serve over rice or noodles.

Caprese Salad

Slice fresh tomatoes and mozzarella cheese. Arrange them on a plate, alternating tomato and cheese slices. Sprinkle fresh basil leaves over the top. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze.

Vegetarian Chili

In a pot, combine canned beans, diced tomatoes, and frozen mixed vegetables. Add chili powder, cumin, and salt to taste. Simmer for 15-20 minutes until heated through. Serve with shredded cheese on top.

Homemade Pizza

Spread pizza dough on a baking sheet. Top with canned tomato sauce, shredded cheese, and your favorite toppings. Bake according to dough instructions until crust is golden.
